TCP/IP

Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol (TCP/IP) is the fundamental communication framework used for transmitting data across networks, including the internet. TCP is responsible for breaking data into packets, ensuring they are delivered accurately and in the correct order, while IP handles addressing and routing each packet to its destination. Together, they ensure reliable and efficient communication between devices regardless of their location or network structure.
